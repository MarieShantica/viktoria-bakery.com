    • Our History

    Initially, VIKTORIA was the only department store located in the Chinatown of Magelang. With full address Jl. Youth no 23, Long Village, Central Magelang District, Magelang city. Toko Viktoria was founded by parents, Mr. Ted Millard Lumanauw in 1941 which provides a variety of goods such as cosmetics, perfumes, clothes and food.

    And then the Viktoria shop turned into a bakery by changing its name to Hollan Bakery and then a few years later it changed its name again to Viktoria Holan Bakery until now.
